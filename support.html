Support

Having troubles with Spendee? Check our support page or get in touch with us to solve any doubts or questions you might have.

Faq

General

Is the Upgrade to the Premium version free for users who have bought the old Spendee app?

Users who have bought the previous version of Spendee app (1.xx) are getting all the previous features within the new Spendee, furthermore the synchronization feature, the ability to associate place with a transaction or the ability to add and edit category from the add transaction screen are available for them for free too.

The upgrade to a Premium version is charged based on our subscription model. We have decided to make the premium features available for everyone for a little monthly fee, which will greatly help us to put more effort into the Spendee development works so other features and Spendee apps for other platforms can be available soon.

How do I cancel the subscription to the Premium?

It’s unfortunate to hear that you want to refund your Spendee. To do so please refer directly to the App Store’s / Google Play’s refund policy. See the links below:

App Store’s refund policy
returning a paid app on Google Play

You need to ask them for a refund, not us. We are unable to help you in this matter.

Are custom categories available in Spendee? How do I create them?

Spendee can be customized exactly to your needs by adding multiple custom categories. Simply add a custom category in wallet’s detail or while adding a new transaction. Select an appropriate icon from a wide icon set, choose from one of the predefined colors and give your custom category a name.

I cannot login, I have forgotten the password of my Spendee account. How do I reset it?

Simply tap on the forgotten password link which appears on the login screen. Fill in your email address. Now check your inbox for an email from us. Open the link that we’ve just sent over to you. The link will lead you to an online password reset page where you set your new password. We require you to insert password twice for its confirmation.

I have forgotten my 4-digit PIN code that I have set up to protect my Spendee transactions. How do I reset it?

The PIN code recovery feature is not available. You need to reset your app’s cache and data (this is done in settings of your phone -> app management -> Spendee) or just simply reinstall the whole app.

How do I get the latest information about Spendee?

For the latest news about Spendee and for the information regarding the coming soon features, please follow our social media channels:
Facebook: https://www.facebook.com/spendeeapp
Twitter: https://twitter.com/spendeeapp

Can I downgrade to the previous version?

No, you cannot downgrade from Spendee 2.XX to the old version 1.XX. We have decided to not support such option as the new version has a lot more to offer and has an overall better UI.

How do I delete my Spendee account?

To delete your Spendee account, please contact our support at spendee@cleevio.com , and we will assist you with your request.

Transactions

I would like to view all my transactions from a specific period. Is the custom view range available?

Yes, absolutely. Both on your Spendee timeline and in the overview mode transactions can be either viewed in the predefined viewing ranges or you can define your own custom periods by setting the From and To dates.

Is it possible to associate places with my transactions?

Additional information like the place where the transaction has been made are easy to insert. While adding a new transaction simply tap on the place icon to select the desired place. Associating places with your transactions will enable you the option to view all your transactions on the map in the overview mode.

I want Spendee to remind me to add transactions, how do I do it?

To enable this feature, you first need to allow Spendee push notifications. Then add a future or repeated transaction. In the transaction detail set a reminder by tapping on the alarm clock icon. Eventually in Spendee app you can go to Settings -> Reminder to select a desired reminder time.

How do I access the pie chart for my expenses?

To access the pie chart you first need to go to the overview mode of your desired wallet. Once there, open the filter options. If on iOS then open it by tapping on the upper right corner icon. If on Android then open the drop down menu by tapping on the top bar item or the little white arrow next to it. Select categories as your desired viewing scope. Now Spendee will display the category pie chart for the selected period.

Wallets

How to create multiple accounts/wallets within the Spendee app?

In Spendee Premium you can add as many wallets as you want to. To add a new wallet open the left side panel i.e. by tapping on the menu icon in the upper left corner. If on iOS then tap on Create New Wallet field on the menu screen. If on Android then continue by tapping on the currently selected wallet to see all your Spendee wallets. Once there, choose the option to Create New Wallet to activate the new wallet’s wizard.

Can I set an initial wallet’s balance to start with?

Sure, initial balance can be either set while creating new wallet or anytime from the wallet’s edit screen.

Which currencies are supported?

Spendee and its wallets support literally any currency you desire. Simply select the wallet’s default currency while creating your new wallet.

DATA SYNC, TRANSFER & BACKUP

Does Spendee support a feature of linking two or more users together? Can Spendee handle multiple users accessing the same account (i.e. husband and wife)?

Yes. In Spendee Premium wallets can be shared with other Spendee users simply by selecting the specific wallet, by tapping on Members and by providing information that specifies accounts of other Spendee users.

I have changed phones. How do I transfer my Spendee transactions to my new device?

The "transfer" is very easy and user friendly and is available for all registered / logged in users. The only thing you need to do is to open the app on your new phone and use your current Spendee account to log in. Once logged in your data will be loaded automatically.

When I install the new Spendee 2.0, will my old data be transferred from the old Spendee to the new version?

Yes, your old data from version 1.XX will be safely transferred into Spendee 2.0.

How does the sync feature work?

The new Spendee no longer supports iCloud synchronization. Instead our servers are used for the sync purposes. Such solution not only gives you ability to sync your Spendee data across multiple platforms and multiple devices but it also enables you to share your selected wallets with other Spendee users.

Is there a way to export / import transactions?

We have decided to no longer support these features in the Spendee app. We feel that they are not needed any more as all data are always backed up on our servers. Furthermore we plan to introduce a web-based Spendee too, which would allow users to view their transactions online no matter the device they would want to access them from.

Can I import data from an Excel file, QFX or CSV?

Data import of these files into Spendee is not yet supported. This feature will be available once the planned web-based Spendee will be released.

Can I restore my old Spendee data backed up on iCloud to the new Spendee 2.0?

No, unfortunately this option is not possible.

COMING SOON FEATURES

Will you make a web version of Spendee?

Yes, the web version of Spendee is part of our short term plan and will be available online soon.

Is it possible to make transfers between different wallets?

The ability to transfer funds between your Spendee wallets is coming soon.

Will you make an iPad / WP version?

Yes, the iPad version of Spendee is planned and will be released in the future. On the other hand though the WP version will not be developed.

Is a budget feature supported?

This feature is coming soon.

Does Spendee support a “travel” wallet?

So called “travel” wallet allowing users to add transactions in single wallet in multiple currencies is a feature which will be supported by Spendee soon.

Is a direct link between Spendee and bank account included in the app?

Currently the direct data import from your bank account is not available however we plan to support such functionality within the future Spendee update.

Any questions?

If you didn’t find the awswer to your question, get it touch with us.
